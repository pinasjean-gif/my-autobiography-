My Autobiography

Life is a journey full of dreams and lessons that guide us and help us to grow. Everyone has a story and mine is still in the progress as I continue each day and I am the person with big hopes for my future. My name is Jean V. Piñas, I just recently turned 19 last October 12. I live in Bankal, Lapu-Lapu City. I am currently studying at Lapu-Lapu City College as an Information Technology student. Being a student is not always easy but I am willing to work hard because I know this will help me reach my dreams.

I am the youngest child among three siblings. Growing up I experienced both being cared for and also being challenged to prove myself. I have always love animals, especially cats because they make me feel happy and relaxed whenever I play with them. Aside from that, I also enjoy reading fiction books and watching dramas. These simple hobbies are my stress relievers whenever I feel tired or bored. It also give me ideas, lessons and inspiration that I carry in my daily life.

I also love exploring new things because I believe learning does not only happen in school but also through experiences. Every new challenge or opportunity I face helps me grow and discover more about myself. My biggest goal is to finish my studies, so I can be successful in the future. I want to show people especially my family that I can achieve my dreams. I want them to feel proud of me and see that all their sacrifices are worth it.

My life is a journey filled with lessons, challenges and dreams. As days, months or years may pass I am more eager to keep striving and learning. Life may not always be easy but I believe that with patience and determination, I will reach the future that I have been hoping for. This is who I am and I will continue to do my best until I make my dreams come true.
